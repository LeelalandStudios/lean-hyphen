# 🤖 DEEPFAKES & AI SCAMS

*If you can see it and hear it — it still might not be real.*

## How the trick works

AI copies a real person's face and voice → creates a convincing video or audio clip → you trust it because it looks genuine → you click a link or send money → they take everything.

*Your brain is wired to trust faces and voices. Scammers now have tools to fake both.*

## The red flags

- 🔴 Famous person doing a giveaway through an external link
- 🔴 Channel or account is very new with very few posts
- 🔴 Asking for UPI PIN or password to "receive" money
- 🔴 Urgency — "only X spots left," "expires in 10 minutes"
- 🔴 Threatening messages claiming to have photos or videos of you

## Real example

**Fake Dhoni video on YouTube**

"Invest ₹500, I'll send ₹2,000 back. Tap the link below."

*Channel: created 6 days ago. 2 videos.*

## What you do

- ✦ Check the channel or account age before trusting any video
- ✦ Real celebrities never do giveaways through random links or ask for your PIN
- ✦ If someone threatens you with AI-generated content — tell a trusted adult immediately. Do not pay.
