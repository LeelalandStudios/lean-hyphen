# 🎮 GAMING & SOCIAL MEDIA SCAMS

*They know what you love. They use it against you.*

## Why these specifically

Scammers study teenagers. They know you care about your rank, your skins, your followers, your account. They engineer the exact threat — or prize — that makes you act before you think.

## How the trick works

Free currency or followers offered → fake "official" account or admin → asks for login "to deliver the gift" OR sends a threat about your account being banned.

## The red flags

- 🔴 Free V-Bucks, Robux, Diamonds, followers — anything that costs real money
- 🔴 "Admin" messaging you in-game or on Instagram
- 🔴 "Your account has been reported — verify in 24 hours"
- 🔴 Asking for username AND password "to gift you"
- 🔴 "Download this mod for unlimited coins"

## Real example

**Instagram DM from @insta_verify_team_official**

Your account has been flagged. Verify here in 24 hours or it will be permanently deleted. → instagram-verify-account.net

*Instagram's real warnings appear inside the app — never through a DM with an external link.*

## The truth

- ✦ Free currency generators don't exist. Never have.
- ✦ Real platforms send warnings inside the app — not through unknown DMs.
- ✦ If someone needs your login to "give" you something — they're taking, not giving.
