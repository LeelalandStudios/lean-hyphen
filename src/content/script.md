**Module:** Money Safety & Scams I — Spot the Scam: Links, OTPs & Gaming Traps

## ACT 1 — STORY

### Scene 1: Homescreen (unchanged)

- Lock → home → app drawer. Dock: WhatsApp, Messages, PhonePe, Amazon, Ludo King.
- End beat: WhatsApp activity on home (group notification).

### Scene 2: Friends group + infographic (new)

- Zack forwards ₹5,000 Paytm cash scam to **Aryan, Mei, Zack, Priya**.
- Dialog: Zack asks to click; Aryan urges hurry; Mei is unsure; **Priya warns — scam**.
- Priya shares **SPOT THE SCAM!** infographic; player can view and save to phone.

### Act 3 — Scenario 1 (PhonePe trap)

Setup → choice on Messages thread.

**Path A:** Chrome fake login → PhonePe ₹0 → group (you lost money) → Priya (link red flags).

**Path B:** Delete SMS → group (Zack lost ₹3,000) → Priya (pattern).

**Path C:** Forward in group → angry (Aryan lost, your fault) → Priya (don't forward).

### Zomato mini (after Scenario 1, before Scenario 2)

Group orders food → Aryan forwards ₹500 Zomato voucher scam → single action: ignore and warn group → group agrees; Priya notes same pattern, different logo.

### Scenario 2 — Wrong number OTP

Setup → Paytm OTP + unknown thread + choices. **Path B:** ignore, wallet stays **₹200**, group + Priya. **Wrong path:** send OTP, wallet **₹200 → ₹47** (₹153 debited), group + Priya.

### Scenario 3 — “It’s your friend” OTP (Rafi)

WhatsApp from **unknown number** pretending to be Rafi; Free Fire OTP in Messages. **Call** saved Rafi → verify scam. **Send** OTP → Free Fire drained. **Ignore** → pressure in chat.

### News + share (after Scenario 3)

Teen Wire Inshorts article on friend OTP scam. Player shares to friends group with message tied to their S3 path → group chat → **Priya** concludes.

### Scenario 4 — School email (fake link)

Gmail: `sch00l-portal.com` (zeros). **Tap** → fake portal → **official school email (compromised)** → share that email to group → Priya. **Check** → spot domain → share → Priya. **Forward** → spreads link → share → Priya.

### Scenario 5 — Fake MLBB admin

In-game admin DM, ban threat. **Reply** with password → drained. **Block** → safe. **Tap** verify link → APK trap. Share debrief + Priya per path.

### Act 4 — Spot the Issue game

Priya posts **leelaland.app/spot-the-scam** in the friends WhatsApp group. Player opens the link and taps red flags in 3 rounds (fake school email, OTP SMS, fake game admin). Return to group — Priya concludes.
