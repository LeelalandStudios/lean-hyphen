#### **🃏 CARD 1 — DEEPFAKES & AI SCAMS**

**CARD HEADER:**

🤖 **DEEPFAKES & AI SCAMS** *"If you can see it and hear it — it still might not be real."*

**HOW THE TRICK WORKS:** AI copies a real person's face and voice → creates a convincing video or audio clip → you trust it because it looks genuine → you click a link or send money → they take everything

*Your brain is wired to trust faces and voices. Scammers now have tools to fake both.*

**THE RED FLAGS:**

🔴 Famous person doing a giveaway through an external link 🔴 Channel or account is very new with very few posts 🔴 Asking for UPI PIN or password to "receive" money 🔴 Urgency — "only X spots left," "expires in 10 minutes" 🔴 Threatening messages claiming to have photos or videos of you

**REAL EXAMPLE:**

🎮 **Fake Dhoni video on YouTube** "Invest ₹500, I'll send ₹2,000 back. Tap the link below." *Channel: created 6 days ago. 2 videos.*

**WHAT YOU DO:**

✦ Check the channel or account age before trusting any video ✦ Real celebrities never do giveaways through random links or ask for your PIN ✦ If someone threatens you with AI-generated content — tell a trusted adult immediately. Do not pay.

---

#### **🃏 CARD 2 — OTP SCAMS**

**CARD HEADER:**

🔢 **OTP SCAMS** *"One code. One mistake. Everything gone."*

**WHAT AN OTP ACTUALLY IS:** OTP \= One-Time Password. The 6-digit code sent to verify it's really you. It's the last lock on your account.

**HOW THE TRICK WORKS:** Scammer tries to log into YOUR account → your phone gets the OTP → they contact you pretending to be someone else → you hand them the key → they walk straight in

**THE RED FLAGS:**

🔴 Anyone asking you to share an OTP — for any reason 🔴 "I sent it to you by mistake" 🔴 "Account will be locked in 5 minutes" 🔴 "Support team" on WhatsApp or Telegram asking for OTP 🔴 "Friend" messaging from an unknown number

**REAL EXAMPLE:**

📱 **Garena Support (Unknown Number)** Unusual login on your account. Reply with OTP to verify it's you.

*The real Garena sent that code to protect you. They will NEVER ask you to send it back.*

**THE ONE RULE THAT CANNOT BREAK:**

🟡 **NEVER share your OTP with anyone.** Not a stranger. Not a "friend." Not "support." Not a "bank official." **No company. No game. No human. Ever.**

---

#### **🃏 CARD 3 — GAMING & SOCIAL MEDIA SCAMS**

**CARD HEADER:**

🎮 **GAMING & SOCIAL MEDIA SCAMS** *"They know what you love. They use it against you."*

**WHY THESE SPECIFICALLY:** Scammers study teenagers. They know you care about your rank, your skins, your followers, your account. They engineer the exact threat — or prize — that makes you act before you think.

**HOW THE TRICK WORKS:** Free currency or followers offered → fake "official" account or admin → asks for login "to deliver the gift" OR sends a threat about your account being banned

**THE RED FLAGS:**

🔴 Free V-Bucks, Robux, Diamonds, followers — anything that costs real money 🔴 "Admin" messaging you in-game or on Instagram 🔴 "Your account has been reported — verify in 24 hours" 🔴 Asking for username AND password "to gift you" 🔴 "Download this mod for unlimited coins"

**REAL EXAMPLE:**

📱 **Instagram DM from @insta\_verify\_team\_official** Your account has been flagged. Verify here in 24 hours or it will be permanently deleted. → instagram-verify-account.net

*Instagram's real warnings appear inside the app — never through a DM with an external link.*

**THE TRUTH:**

✦ Free currency generators don't exist. Never have. ✦ Real platforms send warnings inside the app — not through unknown DMs. ✦ If someone needs your login to "give" you something — they're taking, not giving.
